About JS Pvt. Industrial Training Institute, Palhipatti, Varanasi (U.P.)

JS Pvt. Industrial Training Institute, located in Palhipatti, Varanasi, is a recognized private vocational training institute established in 2007. The institute is affiliated to NCVT (National Council for Vocational Training) and functions under the guidelines of the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Government of India. The institute aims to provide high-quality technical and skill-based education that prepares students for employment in various industrial sectors.

Our Mission

  • To provide excellent vocational education & industrial training
  • To prepare students for government and private sector jobs
  • To promote skill development and self-employment
  • To develop professionally trained manpower for industries

Why Choose JS ITI

  • Experienced and qualified trainers
  • Practical and industry-oriented training
  • Well-equipped labs and computer classrooms
  • Personality development and soft skills training
  • Placement assistance and career guidance

Courses Offered

COPA (Computer Operator & Programming Assistent)

  • Basic of computer & MS Office
  • Web desiging ( HTML, CSS)
  • Networking fundamentals
  • Internet & Email

Electrician

  • Electrical wiring & installation
  • Motors and transformers
  • Safety & measuring instruments
  • Commercial & residential electrical work

Fitter

  • Workshop safety and use of hand tools
  • Fitting operations like filing, cutting, shaping, drilling, tapping, assembling metal parts
  • Operating workshop machines (lathe, drilling, grinding)
  • Sheet metal and pipe fitting
  • Basic welding and metal joining techniques
  • Mechanical assembly and maintenance of machines

FDT

  • Basics of fashion, fabrics, colors, and garment types
  • Garment construction: cutting, stitching, sewing, finishing
  • Pattern making and draping techniques
  • Embroidery and decorative work on fabrics
  • Fashion illustration and design sketches
  • Basic use of computer-aided design (CAD) in fashion
  • Employability skills: presentation, teamwork, and professionalism
